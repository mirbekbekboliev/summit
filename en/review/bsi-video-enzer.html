#bSIDUS19-Presentations: Mark Enzer, Mott MacDonald: Digital Twins for Infrastructure

This time Mark Enzer, Chief Technical Officer at Mott MacDonald, presents the British vision of the national Digital Twin for infrastructure in his keynote speech at the buildingSMART International Standards Summit. Over the next 30 years, the various Digital Twins in different industries are to be combined into a single nationwide ecosystem. In this "system of systems", the infrastructure can be operated, maintained and used more efficiently. The basis for the smart infrastructure is secure and effective data management and the equation of digital and physical assets.
