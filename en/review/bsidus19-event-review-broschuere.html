Event-Review: Brochure looks back at highlights of five days in Dusseldorf

Just in time for the half-time between the buildingSMART International Standards Summit in Düsseldorf and the upcoming event in Beijing, we look back at the highlights of the event in Düsseldorf in a brochure. You can find the download link in the article below.
With more than 1,000 participants from over 30 nations, the buildingSMART International Standards Summit and the 17th buildingSMART User Day in Düsseldorf were a complete success. In the brochure we look back on five intensive days in Düsseldorf. On the 63 pages we show some of the highlights of this extraordinary buildingSMART week. In addition, we will briefly discuss an important part of international cooperation - the workspaces at buildingSMART International. It's worth taking a look!

The next buildingSMART International Standards Summit will take place on 29 October 2019 - 1 November 2019 in Beijing. Further information of this event will soon follow on the website of buildingSMART International.
