 Workshop "In practice: openBIM-Workflow"

... is guest at 17th buildingSMART User Day!

Participation is open to all visitors who are in possession of a valid day ticket. About two years ago, an initiative of software companies and system houses came together under the title "Praxis openBIM-Workflow" to demonstrate the exchange of information in Open-BIM projects along the work processes between different trades and disciplines. The workshop, which is coordinated by ccBIM e.V. from Aachen, will be a guest at this year's buildingSMART User Day with two different teams for "Building Construction" and "Infrastructure". The workshop illustrates the BIM process and current possibilities of BIM integration with software products from different vendors. The focus will be on one common BIM model (building or infrastructuer), on the basis of which the individual perspectives and BIM applications will be presented in a comprehensible and practical way. The participants will experience how the actors involved interact in the digital process of "planning, building and operating" and how they can use existing data and expand it in a useful way.
