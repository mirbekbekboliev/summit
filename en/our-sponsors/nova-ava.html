NOVA Building IT GmbH
Freethinkers with Tradition

The business idea is quite simple: NOVA Building IT GmbH develops real innovations. With ultra-modern technology, the right dose of vision, fresh interface designs and the best user experience, the NOVA AVA BIM building software is explicitly aimed at digital natives and digital young-at-heart architects, civil engineers and project controllers. A target group is consciously served that wants to handle complex programs simply and intuitively and expects state of the art products even for specialized software.The company was founded in 2014 with the firm intention of swirling up the previously solid but somewhat static structure of the construction software industry. And NOVA Building IT GmbH has been doing this very successfully ever since: With NOVA AVA BIM, 3D model-based construction cost management has arrived on the web as a pure online service for the first time.

https://avanova.de/

The advantages of location-independent and mobile data acquisition with real-time communication and integrated control options are now available - this simplifies processes, increases productivity and saves resources.A growing network of experts from the BIM and GAEB sectors has formed around the NOVA team. In addition, there are BSI-certified specialists who guarantee compliance with the necessary data protection guidelines and security arrangements. These open structures ensure that the further development of the NOVA AVA BIM platform always involves top specialists from sub-disciplines in addition to the curious, innovative and experienced permanent employees.
