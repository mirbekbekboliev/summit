C/F Data Systems

The Construction Financial Software System built on a time tested reliable platform to bring you 5D BIM.C/F Data systems was founded in the late 70s as a regional Specialty Contractor Accounting package for small business. Now C/F services over 450 companies in 48 states in the USA. Over 10 years ago, a certain specialty contractor liked the program so much they bought the company. This turned out to be more than a marriage of convenience as this union brought synergies to both companies and industry insight to the application itself. C/F serves every trade on the construction site in addition to companies that have nothing to do with construction. The product is that good!

http://cfdatasystems.com/

Projects managed on C/F's platform range from the very small all the way up to Boston's $15B Big Dig. How is that possible? CASim, the award winning user interface developed for contractors is now being used by top contractors to manage productivity and issues in the field. With its included scheduling (4D) and links to the model via third party interfaces, 5D is truly upon us. The initial napkin concept has come full circle to be tied into the model with costing as a natural byproduct. Does your accounting software vendor know about BIM, or buildingSMART International and the requirement for standards? If your program does not adhere to open standards, you can't own your data. It is that simple. You are at the mercy of that system and of course their financial viability. The time is ripe for bringing Building Standards to the one required "app" for the Built Environment: The Accounting System.
Rapaport and Lord are going to demonstrate CASim, the award winning premium executive tool and unveil the PARTie schema for Interior Partitions to be introduced to buildingSMART.

As a visitor to the 17th buildingSMART User Day or the International Summit, you can benefit from the company's know-how. We are looking forward to two exciting reports from the BIM experts of NOVA AVA in the context of the lecture series "BIM at is Best!
