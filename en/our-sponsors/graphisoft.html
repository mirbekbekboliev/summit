ARCHICAD – GRAPHISOFT’s Software for Architects

GRAPHISOFT launched ARCHICAD, the first 3D model-based planning software for architects, in 1984. Known today as Building Information Modeling — or BIM — software, ARCHICAD is highly intuitive thanks to its user-friendly interface. It allows users to work within a central 3D model and to automatically create all the necessary drawings and calculations.ARCHICAD is always state-of-the-art thanks to regular updated versions. Pioneering technologies and new functions making every day work easier are introduced as well. The exchange of 3D data with other stakeholders, such as structural engineers, owners, building technicians or energy consultants is fast and easy through connections to other solutions. open BIM is an important component in all developments and with (among other things) our advanced IFC interoperability we can share and exchange relevant data with everyone. More information at: www.graphisoft.com/archicad

Szabolcs KariSenior BIM Consultant, Graphisoft SE
Szabolcs Kari graduated 2014 in Hungary as an Architect MSc. He used to be a designer architect specialized on single family houses. Later he was BIM Manager in a governmental institution, responsible for the Hungarian BIM Standardization processes and GIS-BIM related solutions. Now he is working for GRAPHISOFT as a Senior BIM Consultant. His expertise is open BIM workflow (IFC and BCF), BIM standards, point cloud-based building survey and ARCHICAD consultancy.
