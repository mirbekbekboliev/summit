Component Assembly Systems and Component West
The #1 choice for Complex and Large Interiors projects

Founded in 1964, Component Assembly Systems has grown steadily from a 3 man operation to well over 1500 employees today. Projects range from a Regional Veterans' Administration Hospital in the deep south to Headquarters of Facebook and Apple in California to the recovery effort at 9/11's Ground Zero and its eventual reemergence as One World Trade Center today. How are these far flung projects managed? CASim Online. At over $100 per hour per man, project productivity is a priority and becomes further complicated by the upstream factors of General Contractors and Owners who care little about Subcontractor difficulties. Should Architects be held accountable for flawed plans? CAS/CW can't wait for that day, the clock ticks every day. We are going to show you the tools that points the needle of responsibility at ourselves, the only entity we can control.

http://www.componentassembly.com/index_noflash.php

CAS will discuss the technologies that they put in play including Total Stations, IR Photogrammetry Scanners (not as precise as lasers, but a lot faster and "good enough"), BIM, Industry plug ins that automate the level 5 detailing, Virtual Mock Ups and of course PARTie: The Industry effort funded by CAS to allow Interoperability throughout the Wall industry.CAS is going to show the need for SequentialIE, FinancialIE and LaborpowerIE. This will bring the concept of Building Smart to areas of the business that are real: Where the contractors who install and provide the finished product reside. The closer standards move to the jobsite, the more relevant these tools will be to more companies as each project may have 50 subcontractors. In summary, come join the PARTie and allow CAS/CW to take you to the site, the jobsite trailer and the Executive suite to give you an overall picture of how major US projects work today in our way.
